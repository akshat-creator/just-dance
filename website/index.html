The Game
Welcome to ‘Just Dance’ which is a motion-based dance video game. The objective of the game is to follow the on-screen dance moves and score points by mimicking the dance moves with your body. The game uses pose-estimation techniques to detect player’s movements and then uses that to give them a score. We utilize OpenCV and Tensorflow for carrying out computations on the video feed obtained from the user’s webcam video.
